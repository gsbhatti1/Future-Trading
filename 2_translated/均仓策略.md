Strategy Description

When you encounter a faith coin and you are afraid that you won’t be able to hold on to it, just start a position balancing strategy. It will automatically sell high and buy low, and always maintain a balance between the value of the coin and the value of U.
Only supports currency spot trading
The minimum transaction amount required for 1% of your capital to buy the currency
